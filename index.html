About our band

Hello and welcome! We are 180 to Blink, our goal as a Blink-182 tribute band is to revive the energy and attitude this skate/pop punk band had to offer in the late 90s. We are three talented, energetic, and passionate young men whose aim is to replicate the behavior of Blink-182. The band was established after our first jam session on September 21st, 2023.
Fun fact: we are all drummers! Although obviously we do not all play drums on stage, we have interesting conversations and definitely build our chemistry/relationship on the foundation of percussion. This is a passion we all commit and connect to.
